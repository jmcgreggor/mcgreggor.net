FROM THE CITY


I was born and raised in Philadelphia and lived there for most of my life. Philly is a great city with a rich history, diverse culture, great food, and some of the best, yet misunderstood, sports fans there are. Shortly after coming back from my last deployment with the Air Force, I chose to travel around U.S.



GROWING UP


I grew up with two crazy brothers in a Scotch-Irish and Italian American family. Our extended family was large, and family gatherings were always entertaining. As a part of growing up, heritage was always important as was loyalty to family and friends. I am grateful for all of the friends that I have made over the years and I am proud to be a part of the McGreggor clan.
