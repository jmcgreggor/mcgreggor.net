The upper northwest region of South Carolina, known as The Upstate, is a great place to visit and live. If you are visiting The Upstate, please check out these local places and events.

PLACES & EVENTS


Swamp Rabbit Trail

A scenic, multi-use (walking and bicycling) trail that traverses over 20miles of an historic railroad corridor along the Reedy River, connecting Travelers Rest to the city of Greenville.


Greenville Drive

Based in Greenville, South Carolina, the Greenville Drive are a Minor League Baseball team, affiliated with the Boston Red Sox and a member of the South Atlantic League.


Cafe & Then Some

Cafe & Then Some is an off the wall comedy dinner theater that has good foond and is always fun.


Falls Park

Located in downtown Greenville's Historic West End, Falls Park on the Reed river, is one of Greenville's best places to visit with it's captivating falls, historic buildings, acres of gardens, walking paths connecting to various parts of the city as well as the Swamp Rabbit Trail.


Traveler's rest Farmers Market

This local farmers market is open on Saturday's normally between 8:30am - 12pm fron May through Sept. The market is a great place for fresh meats & vegetables, local art, live music, and kids crafts & activities.


Swamp Rabbit Cafe & Grocery

A neighborhood cafe & bakery featuring local American eats, desserts, and groceries. Set along the Swamp Rabbit trail, the family friendly cafe is a great place to start, end, or take a break from a walk, or ride along the trail. There is also an outdoor pizzaria that extends the lunchtime options.


Living Gallery

An incredibly unique, "dramatic presentation of sacred masterworks of art depicting Christ's ministry and passion". Choirs, musicians, and costumed actors re-create scenes from Christ's ministry on earth. Not much more can be said about this unique performance other than it is oneof the most breathtaking things that one can see on stage and it is well worth the cost (which is not expensive anyway). Note: do not watch any videos that display the performances as it will take the magic away from seeing it live.


Fellowship Greenville

As mentioned above, this highly informative, team led church works to help people understand and build a relationsh with Christ by magnifying grace, loving others, and advancing the Gospel (not religion). As a result people and the church are incredibly welcoming; please visit.


Artisphere

Normally taking place for 3 days in May, "Artisphere is an award winning art festival in Greenville, SC celebrating fine arts, music, food, and fun."
